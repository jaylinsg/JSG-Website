Short-Term Career Goals

I'm hoping to start my career in financial technology at a cooperative, innovation-focused bank. In an entry-level developer or analyst role, I'd like to support enhancements to online banking tools—whether that's helping maintain secure transaction endpoints or improving user-friendly interfaces on mobile and web.

Long-Term Vision

In the next 5-10 years, I aim to take on increasing responsibility within a fintech team—perhaps leading a small project or mentoring interns. Over time I'd like to move into a senior developer or technical coordinator role, where I can help shape the direction of new banking features that make financial services more accessible.

Technical Passions

I've enjoyed designing clean, reliable APIs and working with data models in Django and Android. Going forward, I want to deepen my understanding of cloud-based deployments, microservices patterns, and data analysis techniques for financial applications. I also plan to keep picking up new languages and tools so I can adapt quickly to whatever stack a team needs.

Personal Growth & Impact

Beyond coding, I'm focused on sharpening my communication and teamwork through real-world projects and code reviews. I'd also like to give back by volunteering at local coding workshops or tutoring peers, helping make technology more approachable for others.
